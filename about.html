About Eastone

Eastone was founded to bridge the gap between high-quality construction manufacturing and the local community. We serve as a centralized hub where homeowners and local contractors can access premium materials that were previously only available to large-scale industrial projects.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower local builders by providing not just the materials, but the technical expertise required to build structures that last. We believe that every local home deserves the same level of protection against the elements as the city's largest skyscrapers.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✓ Authentic Brands: Direct from Bostik & Sika.
  • ✓ Local Expertise: Knowledge of local weather issues.
  • ✓ Community Focused: Support for small contractors.
  • ✓ Fast Delivery: Same-day local hauling available.
